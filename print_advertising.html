Publisher's Weekly

Fondly referred to as “the bible of the book business”, Publisher’s Weekly is a weekly news magazine focused on the publishing business for books all over the world. Its website, www.publishersweekly.com, entertains an average of 8 million visitors a year and receives up to 2 million views per month. With your book being placed in the site, it has the potential to reach thousands of the magazine’s subscribers in the web, media, literary agents, bookstores, libraries, and even movie and studio executives.
